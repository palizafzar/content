Face First

The Face First app enables users to identify individuals remotely. This online face recognition app is ideal for law enforcement, retail, security agencies, and transportation hubs.

It offers a full spectrum of surveillance, customer interaction, mobile access control, and forensic facial recognition capabilities. Face First also provides robust APIs and SDKs for seamless integration into diverse platforms and systems.

Railer

Railer is a mobile-based facial recognition attendance system. It allows educational institutions and corporations to manage the check-ins and check-outs of students and employees using advanced facial recognition technology.

This app also offers analytics, mobile notifications, and timely attendance reports. Additionally, it manages leaves such as sick leave, daily leave, public holidays, and more, making it a comprehensive HR solution.

TrueKey

Developed by McAfee Security, TrueKey leverages biometric technology to safeguard and manage your passwords using facial recognition or fingerprint authentication. It employs AES-256 encryption and multi-factor authentication to secure your data against unauthorized access.

BioID

BioID, a German biometric company founded in 2007, specializes in live facial detection and recognition software. Its solutions include PhotoVerify, which confirms document ownership biometrically for remote identity proofing.

BioID offers anti-spoofing capabilities, document ownership verification, e-signatures, and multi-factor authentication via its cloud-based API services.

MojiPop

MojiPop, developed by Eureka Studio in the UK, uses facial recognition to create customized avatars. Users can generate interactive stickers and personalize avatars with various hairstyles, backgrounds, and expressions.

This app supports 58 languages, ensuring accessibility for a global audience. Simply take a selfie, and MojiPop does the rest!

Face2Gene

Developed by the AI-driven company FDNA, Face2Gene is a healthcare app designed for medical professionals to identify genetic disorders using deep learning algorithms based on syndrome gestalt analysis.

DigiPass

Developed by OneSpan, DigiPass employs dual authentication, combining facial recognition and fingerprint scans for enhanced digital security. This feature ensures protection against malware attacks and supports multi-platform compatibility, including Android, iOS, Windows, and Linux.

AppLock

AppLock uses facial recognition and voice authentication to secure mobile apps, ensuring that only authorized users access sensitive information like social media and financial accounts.

Blippar

Founded in 2011, Blippar is a leading AR technology company leveraging deep learning to identify real-world objects through smartphone cameras. It provides an interactive AR experience that overlays digital content onto the physical world.

Luxand

Founded in 2005, Luxand specializes in facial feature recognition solutions used in biometrics, security, banking, entertainment, and more. Its technologies are widely adopted for law enforcement, military identification, and automated document workflows.
