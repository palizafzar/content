The Biometric Summit London 2019 on Wednesday 20 November follows on from the Summit in New York in Spring 2019 building on the success of the Biometric Summit London 2018. New for this Summit is further sessions and engagement on biometrics for digital identity verification, electronic Know Your Customer, retail and IoT deployment. Hosted by Rise London, the key themes of the event will be biometric technology and digital identity with the aim of bringing together speakers and delegates for a thought leading and educational event through enlightening talks, panels and workshops.

With up to 120 senior leaders from a range of sectors such as finance (including the FinTech community), government, manufacturing, technology and this power-packed summit provides plenty of opportunity for making new contacts. This is an outstanding opportunity to learn from experts and end-users, bringing together investors and buyers interested in discovering the latest innovation in biometrics and digital identity.

Biometric technology and digital identity specialists will be on hand with expert knowledge and case studies to share the latest innovation in these areas. This will enable you to deepen your understanding and help you make decisions on the best strategy to deploy biometric technology in your organization.

Themes and topics:

Biometrics technology in Payments including:

  • The opportunity for biometric payment cards
  • Are you ready for naked payments? (No card, no token, no phone)
  • Goode Intelligence shares its latest research into Biometrics in Payments

The role of regulation in biometrics including:

  • Biometric Identity and Blockchain
  • Latest developments in biometric
  • Biometrics for next-generation retail (in-store)
  • Behavioral biometrics and fraud management
  • Biometrics for the IoT including the connected car

Digital identity

  • Digital Identity landscape for FinTech
  • The role in eKYC in supporting digital onboarding
  • Is the time right for Sovereign ID?
  • One identity to rule them all – Fact or Fiction?
  • Risk-based authentication for eCommerce and payments

The attendees are:

  • Industry analysts, including access to Alan Goode, one of the world’s leading authorities on biometrics
  • CEOs of technology companies
  • End-users and experts to gain insight into what and where they are deploying biometric technology
  • Investors with a proven track record in biometric technology

What you can expect:

  • Attend a tailored program of enlightening and interactive talks, workshops and panels to build and strengthen your knowledge about deploying biometric technology in your organization and its optimum use for authentication, fraud management, identity verification and more
  • Hear from the experts about what the latest trends are and their implications for FinTechs
  • Engage in discussions with leading players from the digital identity, FinTech and banking industries
  • Participate in authoritative streams that will give you insights into these fast-moving markets, their emerging challenges, and opportunities
  • Easily compare and benchmark potential products, services, and solutions
