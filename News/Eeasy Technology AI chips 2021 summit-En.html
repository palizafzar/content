About Eeasy Technology | 亿智电子

Eeasy Technology is a system solution provider focused on artificial intelligence machine vision algorithms and SoC (System on a chip) chip design. The company is commended to intelligent empowerment in the video surveillance, automotive electronics, and smart hardware markets. It has scaled up the final AI SoC chips production, incorporated a self-designed neural network processing unit NPU, and built a full-stack AI chip solution.

Eeasy Technology Co. Ltd. introduced high-performance security AI SoC chips SV826 and SV823/SV823C. For smart security scenarios, SV826 and SV823/SV823C use smart H.265+ coding technology to support up to 4K ultra-high-definition video recording; at the same time, it integrates a professional security-level ISP, supports 2~3 frame wide dynamic fusion and adaptive noise reduction. Excellent performance in backlight and low-light environments; equipped with the second-generation self-developed NPU of Eeasy, providing 1.5T/1.0T intelligent computing power.

SV826/SV823/823C can be combined with a variety of third-party algorithm applications, such as "machine-not-human" detection and recognition, high-altitude parabolic, electric vehicle recognition, helmet/helmet recognition, and other object recognition algorithms to help smart security applications land.

As a pioneer in end-side AI chips, Eeasy Technology is determined to independent research and development of fundamental AI chip technologies. Yizhi Electronics has already empowered millions of smart electronic devices with AI chips, establishing a leadership position in the industry. By focusing on AI application strategies, prioritizing customer needs, and following market demand as a technical guide, Eeasy Technology continues to iterate AI chip products to enable general computing power for end-side AI chips, enabling AI technology to serve thousands of industries and empower trillions of terminals.
