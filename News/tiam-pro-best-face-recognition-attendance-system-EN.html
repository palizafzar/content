Key Features and Specifications

The Tiam Pro biometric attendance device integrates innovative technologies, ensuring not only high speed and accuracy in facial recognition and fingerprint authentication but also robust security. Below is a closer look at its standout features:

1. 99.7% Recognition Accuracy

Tiam Pro boasts an impressive 99.7% accuracy rate in facial and fingerprint recognition. This ensures reliable identification, minimizing potential fraud. Such precision makes it particularly suitable for environments demanding high security, such as government institutions or large corporations.

2. Live Facial Detection

Utilizing advanced algorithms, Tiam Pro distinguishes between live faces and static images. This anti-spoofing technology reduces the likelihood of fraud using photos or videos, reinforcing secure access control.

3. Exceptional Speed

Tiam Pro excels with a face recognition speed of under 0.2 seconds. This rapid processing eliminates delays, allowing managers to control employee movements efficiently and seamlessly.

4. Multi-Person Recognition

The device is capable of recognizing up to five faces simultaneously, making it ideal for high-traffic environments such as factories, universities, or large offices. This feature reduces congestion at entry and exit points, enhancing operational flow.

Optical and Lighting Adaptation Technologies

One of the common challenges in facial recognition systems is performance in varying lighting conditions. The Tiam Pro addresses this with infrared and white light technology, ensuring accurate recognition even in dimly lit or overly bright environments. This adaptability allows for reliable functionality in diverse workplace settings.

Powerful Linux OS and Advanced Processor

Running on Linux OS version 3.10, the Tiam Pro guarantees stability and optimized performance. Its ARM Cortex A7 processor, equipped with a high-speed 1.2 NPU, handles data processing effortlessly, even during peak workloads.

Extensive Communication Capabilities

Tiam Pro supports diverse communication methods, including TCP/IP, optional Wi-Fi, and USB 2.0. These features enable seamless data transfer and integration with HR management software or other enterprise systems. This versatility ensures a smooth flow of attendance data across various platforms.

7-Inch Touchscreen Display and Advanced UI

The 7-inch HD touchscreen (1280x800 resolution) enhances usability, offering a clear interface for users to interact with the system. The intuitive and user-friendly design ensures an excellent user experience, allowing employees to view their attendance and managers to configure settings with ease.

Compatibility with Smart Cards

Beyond facial and fingerprint recognition, Tiam Pro supports various smart card technologies, including:

  • 125KHz EM cards
  • 13.56MHz smart cards
  • Felica, DESFire, and HID cards

This flexibility makes Tiam Pro suitable for organizations already using smart cards for access control.

Built-in Battery and Emergency Features

A significant advantage of the Tiam Pro is its built-in battery, which keeps the system operational during power outages. This ensures uninterrupted performance, even in emergency situations.

Compact and Stylish Design

With dimensions of 120x242x30 mm, the Tiam Pro is compact and easy to install in various locations. Its modern design seamlessly integrates with any office décor while occupying minimal space.
