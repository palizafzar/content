Palizafzar at IPAS 2025 – Showcasing the Future of Biometric Access Control and Smart Security

Palizafzar, a leading Iranian provider of biometric and smart access control technologies, is pleased to announce its participation in the 22nd International Police, Safety, and Security Equipment Exhibition (IPAS 2025). The exhibition will be held from October 13 to 16, 2025 (21–24 Mehr 1404) at Imam Khomeini Mosalla in Tehran, where Palizafzar will welcome visitors at Booth AS13.

Introducing Next-Generation Access Control and Attendance Solutions

During this major industry event, Palizafzar will unveil its latest achievements in biometric access control, time attendance systems, and facial and fingerprint recognition technologies. Visitors will have the opportunity to experience these innovative products firsthand and explore how Palizafzar’s intelligent systems can enhanceorganizational security and efficiency.

Palizafzar’s presence at IPAS 2025 will focus on the company’s new generation of integrated access control platforms—powered by AI-driven biometric verification, real-time monitoring, and cloud-based management software. These solutions provide secure, reliable, and flexible tools for managing entry and exit points in workplaces,industrial facilities, and government institutions.

Innovation Through Biometric Technology

As a pioneer in the biometric technology industry, Palizafzar continues to advance security through innovation.The company’s upcoming lineup includes devices featuring dynamic facial recognition with mask detection, high-speed fingerprint identification, and multi-factor authentication.These features ensure both accuracy and convenience while minimizing the risk of unauthorized access to sensitive environments.
