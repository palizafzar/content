Touchscreen and Interface: A vivid, responsive design

The Tiam attendance device features a 5-inch full-color touch display with a resolution of 1280x720 pixels, making navigation and data entry effortless. Its sleek, touch-sensitive keypad is integrated directly into the screen. Equipped with Wide Dynamic Range (WDR) technology, the Tiam device delivers unparalleled performance even in challenging lighting conditions, such as darkness, intense brightness, or backlit environments.

When a user is identified, the device displays their photo on the screen, offering clear confirmation of successful recognition and attendance logging.

The design incorporates dual Sony cameras to combat fraud attempts using photos or phone screens. Users can adjust the facial recognition distance between 0.8 meters to 2.5 meters, with the system completing the identification process in just 0.2 seconds using a combination of infrared and white light.
