How to Identify Employees by Using Time and Attendance Systems?

When the staff put their finger on the fingerprint attendance device sensor, a time attendance device identifies points where the fingerprint intersects or ends and then creates a map of these points. This coordinate map is used as the biometric personnel for identification. ADP time and attendance devices compare taken fingerprints with available fingerprints in the database. If fingerprints match with preset patterns, the employee is correctly identified, and his clocking in or out will be enrolled. Otherwise, they do not recognize it.
